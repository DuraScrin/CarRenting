    With thousands of locations in over 160 countries, Europcar is the go-to choice for many people in Europe and around the world for car rentals. Indeed, Europcar is a car rental company that offers quality services in Brussels airports and other cities worldwide. If you're on a tourist trip to Brussels, Antwerp or Liège and are looking for a reasonably priced rental car, Europcar is one of the best options available.

    Renting a car with Europcar is simple and easy, whether you do it online or in person at one of our numerous rental locations. We offer a wide range of vehicles, including compact cars that are ideal for city driving, as well as larger cars for trips outside the city. Whether you're on a business trip or vacation, Europcar has a car to meet your rental needs.

    As a major international player in the car rental industry, Europcar provides a transparent, quick, convenient, and flexible car rental service. Among the wide range of available rental cars, you'll find:

    • Standard cars: featuring economical models, compacts, sedans (Audi, Mercedes, BMW...), SUVs, and models suitable for large families or luxury cars.

    • Specialty car rentals: such as Toyota's hybrid models, and Polestar’s electric car models.

    • Van rentals like: minivans, box vans, delivery vans or small vans.

    In addition to quality vehicles and dedicated professional assistance, Europcar offers a host of benefits, including: a secure online booking platform, useful additional options (baby seats, extra driver, GPS, etc.).
